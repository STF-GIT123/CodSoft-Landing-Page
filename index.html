Employment of fashion designers is projected to grow 3 percent from 2022 to 2032,
about as fast as the average for all occupations. About 2,300 openings for fashion designers
are projected each year, on average, over the decade. Many of those openings are expected
to result from the need to replace workers who transfer to different occupations or exit the
labor force, such as to retire.
